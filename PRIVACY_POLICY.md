# Privacy Policy for Banana Countdown

**Last Updated**: 1/1/2026

## Introduction

Banana Countdown is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera and Photo Library Access
- Banana Countdown requires access to your device's camera and photo library to scan banana images
- Images are processed locally on your device and sent to our API server for analysis
- Images are not stored permanently on our servers
- Images are deleted immediately after processing

### Data Collection
- We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location
- We do not track your usage patterns or behavior
- We do not use analytics or tracking services

## How We Use Your Information

- **Image Analysis**: Images you select or capture are sent to our API server to analyze banana ripeness
- **Prediction Results**: The analysis results are returned to your device and displayed in the app
- **No Storage**: Images are processed and immediately discarded - we do not store your images

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties
- Images are only sent to our API server for processing and are not shared with any other services
- We do not use advertising networks or analytics services

## Data Security

- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Images are transmitted securely to our servers
- Images are processed and immediately deleted after analysis

## Children's Privacy

Banana Countdown is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
